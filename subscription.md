# Basic Plan (Entry-level subscription)

**Purpose:** Affordable plan for small businesses/startups.

## User Onboarding
- Email OTP, Google OAuth signup  
- Org creation with logo, GST/TAX, currency setup  

## Users & Roles
- Up to 5 users (Admin, Secondary Admin, Salesman, Biller)

## Core Features
- Billing & invoicing (up to 200/month)  
- Party management with credit balance & opening balance  
- Item management (stock, category, unit)  
- CSV bulk upload of items  

### Sales Workflow
- Quotation → Performa Invoice → Invoice → Packing Slip  

### Purchase Workflow
- Purchase → Pay Out → Purchase Return → Expense tracking  

## Reports
- Sales  
- Purchase  
- Day Book  
- Party Statement  
- Stock Summary  
- Expense Report  

## Settings
- Business currency  
- GST/TAX enable  
- HSN/SAC code  
- Place of supply  
- Stop sale on negative stock  
- 2 invoice layouts (Tally style & color header)  

## Support
- Email support  

## Limitations
- No advanced analytics (profit & loss, balance sheet)

# Premium Plan (Full-featured subscription)

**Purpose:** Complete solution for scaling businesses.

## User Onboarding
- All signup methods  
- Advanced org setup (financial year, terms, bank details, signature, logo)  

## Users & Roles
- Unlimited users with role-based permissions  
  - Admin  
  - Secondary Admin  
  - Salesman  
  - Biller  

## Core Features
- Unlimited invoices, quotations, purchase orders, expenses  
- Advanced inventory  
- Transaction messages (WhatsApp/email sharing)  
- Advanced sales workflows (returns, credit notes)  
- Advanced purchase workflows (returns, debit notes)  
- Expense categories with detailed reporting  

## Reports
- Profit & Loss  
- Balance Sheet  
- Party-wise P&L  
- Item-wise P&L  
- Low Stock  

## Extras
- Share reports with CA (WhatsApp/email attachments)
